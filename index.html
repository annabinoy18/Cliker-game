    • ABOUT THE GAME

    RED-GREEN-YELLOW is a fun and engaging color-clicking game that challenges your reaction time and coordination. In this game, players interact with three color-coded buttons: red, green, and yellow. The objective is simple: click each button as many times as possible before the timer runs out.

    HOW TO PLAY

    • Click the Buttons: Each button represents a different color and counts the number of times it has been clicked. Click on the red, green, and yellow buttons to increase your score for each color.
    • Score Tracking: Your score is tracked in real-time and displayed next to each button, allowing you to see how many times youâ€™ve clicked each color.
    • Timer: A countdown timer starts at 15 seconds. Keep an eye on the timer to ensure you maximize your clicks before time runs out.
    • Restart: If you want to play again, hit the "Restart" button to reset the scores and timer. You can also return to the home page using the "Home" button.
